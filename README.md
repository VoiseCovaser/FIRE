## üîé What is FIRE?

FIRE is an easy-to-use tool designed to help you plan for financial independence and early retirement. This calculator works well for investors in Europe, especially those using UCITS funds. UCITS funds are common investment options regulated across the EU, making the tool tailored to European tax rules and investment styles.

If you want to understand how long it will take to reach financial freedom or how much you need to save each month, FIRE can help you get clear answers without complicated calculations.

## üìã How to Use FIRE

Once you open FIRE, you'll see a clean and clear interface with fields to enter your financial details.

### Step 1: Enter Your Personal Information

- **Current Age:** How old you are now.
- **Retirement Age Goal:** The age you want to reach financial freedom.
- **Current Savings:** Your total money saved for retirement today.
- **Monthly Income:** Your take-home pay each month.
- **Monthly Expenses:** Your regular spending each month.

### Step 2: Input Investment Details

- **Expected Annual Return:** Estimated yearly return from your UCITS funds (for example, 5%-7%).
- **Monthly Investment:** How much you plan to add to your savings every month.
- **Tax Rate:** The tax percentage applicable to gains or income in your country.

### Step 3: See Results

Click the ‚ÄúCalculate‚Äù button. The app will show:

- The number of years it will take to reach your goal.
- The total amount you need to achieve financial independence.
- A graph showing your savings growth over time.
